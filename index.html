Levelstead

Fitness quests without reckless overtraining.

Levelstead is a local-first fitness RPG for beginner-friendly training paths, hydration, fuel check-ins, recovery, and visible progression.

Privacy Policy Support

Local-first

Profile, quest, reminder, and training logs stay on the device in the current build. No account, analytics SDK, ad SDK, or wearable sync is active.

Safety scope

Levelstead provides general fitness habit support. It does not diagnose injuries, treat conditions, or replace professional care.
